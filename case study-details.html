The Challenge

Magical Smiles, a trusted dental practice, wanted to increase its online visibility, drive more organic traffic, and generate high-quality leads through both SEO and paid search campaigns. The goal was to stand out in a highly competitive local market and ensure a steady stream of new patient inquiries.

Our Approach

We implemented a comprehensive digital marketing strategy focusing on two key areas:
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
    Extensive keyword research targeting high-volume, patient-relevant search terms On-page optimization, technical SEO, and local SEO enhancements High-quality content marketing to support topical authority
  • Google Ads (PPC Campaigns)
    Strategic AdWords campaign setup targeting high-intent keywords Continuous A/B testing of ad creatives and landing pages Conversion tracking and budget optimization to reduce cost-per-lead (CPL)
